About Hotel BT

Located in the heart of Patna adjacent to the city’s famous landmark Gandhi Maidan, Hotel Paayas Patna offers easy access to business districts, government offices and leisure attractions. A convenient 20-minutes drive from the airport or 10-minutes from the railway station will reach you at the destination.

And once here, Paayas, a legacy hotel of over forty years, will welcome you with warm hospitality as well as an assurance that your stay in Patna is comfortable and memorable.
