Hotel Paayas Patna is undoubtedly one of the most celebrated landmarks in Bihar. From the time it first opened its doors in 1978 to welcome Patna residents and international tourists, it has remained an iconic hospitality venue. Paayas is Bihar's first luxury's hotel in the state.
